Strong Roots About Our Community

Thanks to Code First Girls, this lovely website could come to life. The idea for Des Temps was born during Quarantine 2020, while the developing team was reflecting on the COVID-19 pandemic. Here, you can enjoy some fun activities for your stay at home and exchange knowledge with other ladies. We can also feel empathy in the midst of a Global Pandemic (in light of racism and the many Police Brutality incidents that have occured) And if you didn't do as much as you though you would, that is okay too. Des Temps, is for everyone.

We guarantee that you will fall in lust with our Community. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
